About Audivea

It all starts with listening.

Audivea is an independent audio software project dedicated to building tools that help musicians and engineers better understand their sound.

We believe great audio tools should do more than process signals — they should reveal what is happening inside your mix and help you make better creative decisions.

Our Philosophy

Our design philosophy is built around two complementary perspectives.

Dive when you want to explore the deeper details of your sound — uncovering what's really happening beneath the surface.

Ascend when you want clarity and focus — surfacing the key information you need to create with confidence.

What We're Building

By combining precise DSP with clear visual design, Audivea builds plugins that feel both powerful and intuitive for modern music production. We focus on tools that bridge the gap between raw audio data and creative understanding.

Our first product is currently in development.
