About Audivea

Audivea is an independent audio software project focused on building tools that help musicians and engineers better understand their sound.

We believe that great audio tools should do more than process signals — they should reveal what is happening inside your mix and help you make better creative decisions.

Our Philosophy

Our design philosophy is built around two complementary perspectives:

Dive when you want to explore the deeper details of your sound — loudness behavior, dynamics, stereo relationships, and spectral balance.

Ascend when you want clarity and focus on what matters most — surfacing the key information you need to make confident mixing and mastering decisions.

What We're Building

By combining precise DSP with clear visual design, Audivea aims to create plugins that feel both powerful and intuitive for modern music production. We're focused on metering, analysis, and visualization tools that bridge the gap between raw audio data and creative understanding.

Our first product is currently in development. If you'd like to stay updated, reach out at audivea.official@gmail.com.
